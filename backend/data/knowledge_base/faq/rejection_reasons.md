# Why might my application be declined?

If we say no, the typical reasons fall into one of these buckets. We'll always tell you the broad category so you know what to fix.

## Credit profile
- CIBIL below our auto-decline threshold (typically < 650)
- Recent default or settled account in bureau
- Too many recent credit inquiries (signals distress)
- Existing credit utilisation > 80% on revolving facilities

## Income & affordability
- Verified income falls below minimum threshold for your city tier
- FOIR exceeds policy cap even at maximum tenure
- Income mismatch > 25% between bank statements and ITR
- Insufficient income vintage (< 2 ITRs for self-employed)

## Document & verification
- KYC mismatch (name/DOB doesn't reconcile across PAN/Aadhaar)
- Employment cannot be verified
- Bank account in someone else's name
- Persistent OTP failures (suggests phone mismatch)

## Vehicle-related
- Used car older than 7 years
- Vehicle not on our approved list (rare exotic/imported)
- Insurance issues for used car

A decline is not permanent — most issues are addressable. Reapply after the underlying issue clears.
