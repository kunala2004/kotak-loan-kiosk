# Can NRIs apply for a car loan?

Yes, but the process and product are slightly different.

## Who qualifies
- Non-Resident Indian holding a valid Indian passport
- Person of Indian Origin (PIO) with valid PIO/OCI card
- Employed abroad for ≥ 1 year (some products require ≥ 2 years)

## Required documents
- Passport + visa pages + work permit
- Overseas employer letter or business proof
- 6 months' overseas bank statements
- NRE/NRO account in India (for EMI debit)
- Indian co-applicant (mandatory) — typically a resident family member

## Constraints
- Maximum loan amount typically ₹50L (higher needs bilateral approval)
- Tenure capped at 60 months
- LTV capped at 80% for new car
- Rate is 50 bps higher than equivalent resident salaried profile
- Power of Attorney to a resident is recommended for loan operations

## Repayment
EMIs must be debited from an NRE or NRO account. Direct overseas debits are not supported.
