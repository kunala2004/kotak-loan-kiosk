# What if my CIBIL score is below 700?

A score below 700 doesn't automatically disqualify you, but it changes the picture:

- **CIBIL 670–699:** Eligible at higher rate (10.5% p.a. or above) and lower FOIR cap. We may ask for a co-applicant or larger down payment.
- **CIBIL 650–669:** Manual underwriting only. Approval depends on income strength and reason for past credit issues.
- **CIBIL below 650:** Most applications declined. We capture your details and can revisit after 3–6 months of clean credit behaviour.

## How to improve before applying
1. Pay any pending credit card bills in full — single biggest lever
2. Don't apply to many lenders in a short window (each pull dings your score)
3. Keep credit utilisation below 30% on all cards
4. Older accounts help — don't close your oldest credit card

If you're declined, we'll tell you the rough reason category so you know what to fix.
