# How do I close my loan early?

Two paths: full foreclosure (close entirely) or partial prepayment (reduce outstanding).

## Full foreclosure process
1. After 12 EMIs paid, request a **foreclosure quote** via the customer portal or branch
2. We send the exact outstanding + foreclosure charges (if any) within 2 working days, valid for 7 days
3. Pay the quoted amount via NEFT/RTGS to the loan account
4. Hypothecation cancellation is initiated within 7 working days
5. RC update with the RTO is your responsibility — we provide a No-Dues Certificate

## What you receive on closure
- **No-Dues Certificate** — within 5 working days
- **Hypothecation cancellation form** — for the RTO
- **Original RC** (if held by us)
- **Insurance endorsement** removing our financial-interest clause

## Common questions
- **Can I close without foreclosure charges?** Yes if floating-rate (RBI rule for individual borrowers). Fixed-rate carries 3% + GST.
- **Will it affect my CIBIL?** Positively — closed loans in good standing improve your profile.
- **Can I close before 12 EMIs?** Generally no, but case-by-case for hardship.
