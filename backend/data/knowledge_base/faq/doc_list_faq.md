# What documents do I need?

For most digital-first applications, **almost everything is fetched automatically** via Aadhaar eKYC, Account Aggregator (your bank statements with consent), and Income Tax portal (with your OTP).

You'll only physically submit if a digital fetch fails for some reason.

## What you'll keep handy at the kiosk
- **PAN card** — first thing we ask for
- **Aadhaar with linked mobile** — you'll receive an OTP
- **Phone you can receive bank-app notifications on** — for the Account Aggregator consent
- **Vehicle proforma invoice** — from the dealer

## What we fetch in the background
- KYC details (name, DOB, address) → Aadhaar eKYC
- Bank statements (6 months) → Account Aggregator with your one-time consent
- Income (Form 26AS / ITR) → Income Tax portal with your OTP
- Credit score and history → CIBIL/Experian/Equifax bureau pull

## When physical docs are still asked
- Self-employed without GST (we may ask for business proof)
- Used car (RC, valuation, insurance)
- Co-applicant cases
- Anything flagged for manual review
