# I'm self-employed — can I get a car loan?

Yes. Self-employed applicants are evaluated on slightly different criteria than salaried.

## What we look at
- **Last 2 years ITR** showing consistent or growing income
- **Net profit** (not turnover) is the income figure used for FOIR
- **Bank statements** showing business and personal cash flow patterns
- **Vintage** — at least 2 years in current business (3+ for newer industries)
- **GST returns** if applicable (turnover > ₹40L)

## Differences from salaried
- Interest rate is 25 bps higher
- Documentation is heavier (business proof + bank statements ≥ 12 months)
- Maximum age at loan maturity is 65 (vs 60 for private salaried)

## Tips for higher approval odds
- Maintain a clean primary business account — high transaction volumes with stable balances help
- File ITR on time — late filers see lower approval rates
- If recent year saw a dip, attach a brief explanation; auto-decisioning is rule-based but the underwriter reads notes
