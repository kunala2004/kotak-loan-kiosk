# Can I prepay my car loan early?

Yes. After your first 6 EMIs, you can part-prepay or fully foreclose at any time.

## Part-prepayment
- Up to **25% of outstanding** per financial year — no charges
- Above 25% — 2% + GST on the excess
- Reduces tenure (preferred) or EMI (your choice)

## Full foreclosure
- Allowed after 12 EMIs
- **Floating-rate loans: zero charges** (RBI directive on individual borrowers)
- **Fixed-rate loans: 3% + GST** on outstanding principal

## When prepayment makes financial sense
- You have surplus funds earning less than your loan rate (~8.5–11%)
- Early in the tenure — interest component of each EMI is highest in the first 24 months
- Before a major life event (job change, business expansion) where cash buffer matters more than fewer EMIs

We do NOT apply prepayment penalties for floating-rate individual loans even if our policy elsewhere allows them — RBI prohibits this.
