# Can I get a loan for a used car?

Yes, with some additional conditions vs new-car loans.

## Eligibility
- Vehicle age at disbursement: **≤ 7 years**
- Combined `vehicle_age + tenure ≤ 10 years` (we won't fund a 7-year-old car for 5 years)
- Loan-to-Value: 60–80% depending on age (see LTV matrix)
- Tenure cap: 60 months

## What's different
- We require an **independent valuation report** from one of our empanelled valuers
- Insurance must be **comprehensive (not third-party only)** for the full tenure
- For private-party purchases, the seller's PAN and bank account details are required for the disbursal flow
- Used-car interest rates are typically 50–100 bps higher than new-car rates

## What we don't fund
- Cars without a clear RC (transfer pending, dispute, lien)
- Cars that have been in major accidents (insurance write-off history)
- Cars older than 7 years
- Vintage / classic vehicles (separate product line)
