# FOIR (Fixed Obligation to Income Ratio) Caps

FOIR is the percentage of monthly income committed to all EMIs (existing + proposed). The policy cap depends on income band and CIBIL score.

| Net Monthly Income | CIBIL ≥ 750 | CIBIL 700–749 | CIBIL 650–699 | CIBIL < 650 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Below ₹30,000 | 45% | 40% | Manual review | Decline |
| ₹30,000 – ₹75,000 | 55% | 50% | 45% | Manual review |
| ₹75,000 – ₹1,50,000 | 60% | 55% | 50% | 45% |
| Above ₹1,50,000 | 65% | 60% | 55% | 50% |

Verified income (from bank statements + ITR) takes precedence over declared income. If the proposed loan pushes FOIR above the cap, reduce loan amount or extend tenure.
