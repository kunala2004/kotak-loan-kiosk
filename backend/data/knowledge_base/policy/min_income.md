# Minimum Income Requirements

| Employment Type | Minimum Net Monthly Income | Income Source |
|---|---|---|
| Salaried — Tier 1 city | ₹25,000 | Last 3 months payslips + bank credits |
| Salaried — Tier 2/3 city | ₹20,000 | Last 3 months payslips + bank credits |
| Self-employed professional (CA, doctor, lawyer) | ₹40,000 (gross monthly turnover ÷ 12) | Last 2 ITRs |
| Self-employed business | ₹50,000 (net profit ÷ 12) | Last 2 ITRs + bank statements |
| Pensioner | ₹15,000 | Pension order + 6 months bank statement |

Verified income via Account Aggregator must be within 25% of declared income. Bonus and variable pay count at 50% weight unless documented for ≥ 2 cycles.
