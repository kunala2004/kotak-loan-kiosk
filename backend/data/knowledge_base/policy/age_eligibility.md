# Age Eligibility & Loan Maturity

## Minimum age at application
- Salaried: **21 years**
- Self-employed: **23 years**

## Maximum age at loan maturity
- Salaried (private sector): **60 years**
- Salaried (government / PSU): **62 years**
- Self-employed: **65 years**
- Pensioner: **70 years** (subject to pension continuity)

## Implication for tenure
The maximum tenure available is `(max_age_at_maturity - current_age)` years, capped further by the policy maximum of 7 years (84 months) for new cars and 5 years (60 months) for used cars.

## Co-applicant override
If primary applicant is near maximum age, adding a younger earning co-applicant (spouse or adult child) extends tenure based on their age — provided they meet income criteria independently.
