# Required Documents by Employment Type

## Universal (all applicants)
- PAN card
- Aadhaar (with linked mobile for OTP)
- Recent passport-sized photo
- Address proof (Aadhaar acceptable if address current)

## Salaried
- Last 3 months salary slips
- Last 6 months bank statement (salary account)
- Form 16 for the last 2 years OR ITR
- Employment proof (offer letter, ID card, or HR letter)

## Self-employed / Business
- Last 2 years ITR with computation
- Last 12 months bank statements (business + personal)
- GST returns last 4 quarters (if applicable)
- Business proof (Shop & Establishment, GST cert, partnership deed)

## All car-related
- Proforma invoice from dealer
- For used car: RC, valuation report, insurance copy

Most documents are auto-fetched via Aadhaar eKYC, Account Aggregator, and Income Tax portal — physical submission only if digital fetch fails.
