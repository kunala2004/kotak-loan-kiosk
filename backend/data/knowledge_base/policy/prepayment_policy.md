# Prepayment & Foreclosure Policy

## Part-prepayment (during the loan)
- Allowed any time after the **first 6 EMIs** are paid
- Up to 25% of outstanding principal can be prepaid per financial year **without charges**
- Prepayments above 25% in a year: **2% + GST** on the excess amount

## Full foreclosure
- Allowed any time after **12 EMIs**
- Foreclosure charges:
  - Fixed-rate loans: **3% + GST** on outstanding principal
  - Floating-rate loans: **Nil** (RBI mandate)
- No charges if foreclosure is from own funds and applicant is the borrower (RBI directive on individual floating-rate loans)

## Partial vs full
For amounts > 50% of outstanding, system treats as full foreclosure. Recommend customers either prepay ≤ 50% or close fully — partial prepayments between 50% and 100% incur full foreclosure charges.
