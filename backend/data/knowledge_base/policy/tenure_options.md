# Tenure Options & Constraints

## Available tenures
- New car: 12, 24, 36, 48, 60, 72, 84 months
- Used car: 12, 24, 36, 48, 60 months
- Refinance: matched to remaining useful life of vehicle

## Constraints that reduce maximum tenure
1. **Vehicle age cap** — combined `vehicle_age + tenure_years ≤ 10` (used cars). Example: a 3-year-old car can have at most a 7-year tenure.
2. **Applicant age** — see age_eligibility policy
3. **CIBIL band** — sub-720 limited to ≤ 60 months
4. **High-LTV** — if LTV > 85%, tenure capped at 60 months

## Tenure trade-offs
Longer tenure means lower EMI but higher total interest paid. For most customers, the sweet spot is 60 months — balances monthly burden with total cost. Going from 60 → 84 months drops EMI by ~18% but adds ~40% to total interest paid.
