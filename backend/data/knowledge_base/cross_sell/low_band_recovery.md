# Cross-sell fits — Sub-700 CIBIL (rebuild path)

Customers in this band are often the most loyal long-term — they remember who said yes. Cross-sell here is about *helping them rebuild* and converting to higher-band products in 12–18 months.

## Right product mix
- **Secured credit card** (against fixed deposit) — builds credit history with low risk
- **Smaller personal loan** (₹50K–₹1L) at higher rate — good repayment data point for next pull
- **Insurance products only** — don't push more credit until first car loan EMIs are paid cleanly

## Tone for the brief
Empathetic and forward-looking. Frame it as "this loan + 12 clean months changes your credit profile materially." Avoid moralising about past credit issues — the system already priced them in.

## Avoid
- Larger personal loans (worsens their FOIR — counterproductive)
- Top-up loans within first 12 months
- Aggressive insurance bundles (perceived as opportunistic)

## Track
Customers in this band who pay 12 EMIs cleanly should be re-evaluated automatically — most cross 700 CIBIL by month 18 and qualify for full product range.
