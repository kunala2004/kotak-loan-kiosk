# Cross-sell fits — Self-employed business owners

Self-employed customers buying a car often also have business cash-flow needs that fit naturally with their existing relationship.

## Strong fits
- **Business loan / working capital** — pre-eligibility based on the same ITR + bank statements we already pulled
- **OD against current account** — useful for receivables fluctuation
- **GST input credit financing** — short-tenor product for businesses with lumpy GST flows
- **Business credit card** — separates personal vs business spend, important for tax/audit

## Tone for the brief
Acknowledge the business angle. A self-employed buyer often sees the car as a partial business tool (client meetings, vendor visits). Mention that the same documents we just collected unlock the next product without re-submission.

## Watch for
- If recent year ITR shows a dip, hold cross-sell suggestions until the next return is filed
- For new businesses (vintage < 2 years), only the credit card is appropriate
- Avoid pushing personal loan products to business owners — usually a step down
