# Cross-sell fits — Premium car buyer (₹25L+ vehicle)

Premium buyers are typically affluent, often have multiple banking relationships, and respond to *quality of service* far more than *low rates*. Don't lead with discounts.

## Strong fits
- **Wealth management onboarding** — relationship-led, not product-led
- **Locker + premium debit card** as a low-friction relationship deepening
- **Higher comprehensive insurance** with zero-dep + engine cover (industry data: premium car owners under-insure)
- **Home loan top-up** if they own property — typically have unused borrowing capacity

## Tone for the brief
Status-aware, concise, opt-in. Premium customers hate being sold to. Frame products as "available to you, given your profile" — not "we recommend you take."

## Service flags
- Offer dedicated relationship manager contact post-disbursal
- Suggest extended warranty / annual maintenance bundle through the dealer
- Mention zero-touch renewal for insurance and FASTag

## Critical
Do not include processing fee or rate concession claims unless they're confirmed in the customer record. Premium customers cross-check.
