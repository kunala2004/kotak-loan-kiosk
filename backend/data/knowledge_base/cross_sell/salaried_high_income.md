# Cross-sell fits — High-income salaried (₹1L+/month, CIBIL ≥ 750)

Customers in this band are pre-screened for several adjacent products that fit the same risk envelope.

## Strong fits
- **Pre-approved personal loan** up to 3× monthly income at preferential rate (no fresh underwriting needed for 60 days)
- **Credit card with travel rewards** — they typically spend 30–40% on travel/dining
- **Home loan pre-eligibility quote** — most are within 18 months of buying property
- **Term life insurance** — large protection gap is typical (industry data: only 38% of high-income salaried have adequate cover)

## Tone for the brief
Use confident, choice-oriented language. They're not borrowing reluctantly — they're optimising. Highlight the rate concession, the speed of disbursal, and the option to add a co-applicant for tax planning.

## Avoid suggesting
- Gold loan (usually irrelevant; signals downward)
- Two-wheeler loan (segment mismatch)
- Microfinance products
