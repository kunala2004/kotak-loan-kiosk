# Employment verification — red flags

## Salaried — what we check
- Name on payslip matches name on PAN/Aadhaar
- Employer name matches employer in bureau internal_data
- Salary credit on bank statement matches payslip net pay (within 5%)
- TDS deduction visible on bank credit (if applicable)

## Salaried — red flags
- Employer entity not registered with EPFO (suspicious for >50-employee firms)
- Salary paid via personal bank account (proprietorship indicator — should be self-employed)
- Recent join date (< 6 months) without prior strong credit history
- Employer in our internal "watchlist" of high-default firms
- No PF/professional tax deductions on payslip (often signals informal arrangement)

## Self-employed — what we check
- Business name on ITR matches GST cert / Shop & Establishment / partnership deed
- Business address matches across docs
- Business vintage ≥ 2 years
- Net profit (not turnover) used for affordability

## Self-employed — red flags
- Business in NACE codes flagged as high-default (varies — refer to fraud desk's current list)
- Single ITR with sudden jump from prior year (> 50% growth without supporting documents)
- Personal and business accounts heavily commingled
- Recent registration date (< 12 months from application)
